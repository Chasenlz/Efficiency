Master DGDI

A fun personal project. I wanted to make an unofficial (which could maybe become official ?) identity for my master degree class. It was a small challenge I made for all our promotion. Unfortunaterly, we were only 2 to make this challenge. We had no student associations, no identity, no hoodie to wear with pride. Here is my take on my graphic & interactive design master degree identity.

I wanted to create something modern, with a numeric vibe. A identity we could play with, animate in different ways. I really enjoyed making it, especially my DGDI icon, which looks like (vaguely, when you squint your eyes) a smiley face, with a smile and a wink.
